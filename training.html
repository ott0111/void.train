Module 01

Ticket Types

Your first and foremost responsibility as a mod is to efficiently manage the ticket system in Void Esports. We operate with two primary ticket categories, each requiring specific handling protocols.

🎟️

General Ticket

These are created by community members for support inquiries, moderator assistance, or reporting issues. Handle with professionalism and follow escalation procedures for complex matters.

📋

Roster Ticket

Application channel for users seeking to join Void Esports. Initial response must include age verification and direction to appropriate resources. Follow structured onboarding workflow.

📌
Protocol

All ticket responses should begin with a professional greeting and age inquiry. Direct new applicants to the appropriate channels and maintain detailed records of all interactions.

Module 03

Mod Responsibilities & Commands

⚠️ Warn / Report

Moderators are responsible for enforcing server rules. Use the warning system to document infractions officially.

  • Use /warn @user [reason] to issue an official warning on record
  • Always document the reason clearly — vague warnings are invalid
  • Three warnings typically escalate to a temporary mute or ban
  • Use /report to flag issues beyond your authority level

🏖️ LOA (Leave of Absence)

All moderators must formally request time away. Unannounced inactivity is grounds for removal.

  • Submit your LOA request at least 24 hours in advance when possible
  • Notify your direct supervisor and arrange ticket coverage
  • Specify your return date clearly
  • Log your LOA in the appropriate channel using /loa [dates] [reason]

🕐 Shift Management

Shifts ensure 24/7 coverage of the ticket system. Adhering to your assigned shift is mandatory.

  • Log shift start with /shift start and end with /shift end
  • Hand off any open tickets to the incoming moderator with detailed notes
  • Never abandon your shift without notifying a senior moderator
  • Consistent early exits or no-shows result in disciplinary review

📊 Weekly Performance Metrics

Every moderator submits a weekly performance report. This data drives staffing decisions and recognizes top performers.

  • Total tickets handled during the week
  • Average first-response time
  • Number of warnings/reports issued
  • LOA days taken
  • Shift completion percentage
  • Any notable escalations or incidents

Module 04

Ticket Handling Guidelines

Here is how moderators should proceed when handling recruitment and support tickets. Always remain professional and follow the process below.

👋 Professional Greeting

  • Always start with a professional greeting.
  • Avoid informal greetings such as "Yo", "Hi", or "Bro".
  • Examples:
  • "Hello, how may I help you?"
  • "Hello, what can I assist you with today?"

❓ Role Inquiry

  • Ask what role the user is applying for before continuing.
  • Examples:
  • "What role are you applying for today?"
  • "What are you applying for here?"

🔎 Age Verification

  • Always verify the user's age before processing their request.
  • Examples:
  • "May I ask how old you are?"
  • "What is your current age?"

🎭 Role-Specific Instructions

  • @Creative Academy / @Main Creative
    Perfect! Please send two–three clips and @Creative Department will be here shortly to review them.
  • @Academy Roster
    Please link your tracker below with an image of the default dance in lobby.
  • @Semi-Pro Roster / @Pro Roster
    Perfect! Please send your tracker below with an image of the default dance in lobby! @operations will be here shortly to review.
  • @VOID VFX / @VOID GFX
    Please link your previous work or portfolio below and @GFX / VFX Lead will be here shortly to review.
  • @Content Creator
    Perfect! Please link your social media accounts below and @Content Department will review them shortly.
  • @Coach
    How many people have you coached previously? Please link their trackers below.
  • @Void Grinder+
    Unfortunately you do not qualify for any roles. However, we can offer you Grinder+.

    For Grinder+ please do the following:
    • Send a screenshot of using our support creator code "Team.Void"
    • Send a screenshot of changing your Epic Games username to include "Void"
    • Use our Discord "Void" tag

⚠️ Blacklist Format

User: Username of the person
User ID: Discord ID
Reason: Underage
  • Always provide the user's username and Discord ID.
  • Only blacklist when a rule has clearly been violated.

✅ Closing the Ticket

  • Ask the user to change their name to include "Void" and send proof they are using the creator code.
  • Example closing instruction:
  • "Perfect! Please change your name to include 'Void' and send a screenshot of using our code."
  • Once they send the images:
  • "Thank you! Here are your roles! Have a nice day!"

📌 Important: Always maintain professionalism during the entire interaction. Document actions taken and escalate situations to senior staff when necessary.
